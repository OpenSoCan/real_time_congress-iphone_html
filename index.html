Data Sources

Floor Updates

House floor updates are pulled from the House of Representatives Office of the Clerk

Senate floor updates are provided by Republicans.Senate.Gov

Leadership Notices

  • House Majority Whip Weekly Whip Pack
  • House Majority Whip Daily Whipline
  • Office of the Republican Whip Daily Whipping Post and Weekly Whip Notice

News

Links to news stories are provided by the RSS feeds of the following publications:

  • The Hill
  • Hotline On Call
  • The Note
  • Open Congress
  • The Page
  • Politico
  • Washington Post

Upcoming Hearings

Upcoming hearings for the House of Representatives are provided by GovTrack.us

Hearing schedules for the Senate are made available on the official website of the U.S. Senate

Documents

Other than Congressional Research Service reports, which are made available by the Center for Democracy & Technology's Open CRS project, reports are obtained from the official websites of the following offices:

  • Congressional Budget Office
  • Democratic Policy Committee
  • Government Accountability Office
  • Republican Policy Committee
  • Joint Committee on Taxation
  • Office of Management and Budget
  • Republican Cloakroom
  • White House/OMB
